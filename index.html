What does Serum Do for Your Skin?









Serums are lightweight, concentrated skincare products designed to deliver active ingredients deep into the skin. They target specific skin concerns like hydration, brightening, anti-aging, and blemishes, often containing ingredients like hyaluronic acid, vitamin C, or peptides. Due to their potency, serums can improve skin texture, tone, and overall appearance faster than creams or lotions.
